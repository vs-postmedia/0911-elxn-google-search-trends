Where is B.C. looking up the leaders?

Google searches for party leaders from Sept. 3 to Sept. 10.

  • Searches for Liberal leader Justin Trudeau were the most widespread, covering most parts of the province. This may be partly explained by incumbency, where some Trudeau-related searches are connected to politcal or policy issues related to governing rather than the election.

    Scroll for more...

  • Search interest in Conservative leader Erin O’Toole was high in the Kelowna corridor through the Oakanagan, as well as in the north.

  • NDP leader Jagmeet Singh had the highest search interest in the south of the province and on the Island.

  • Most searches for Maxime Bernier, the leader of the People’s Party of Canada, were concentrated in the north of the province and the Kelowna corridor of the Okanagan.

  • Searches for Green Party leader Annamie Paul were largley relegated to the Victoria area and parts of the Lower Mainland.
